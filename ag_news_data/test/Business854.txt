Diebold Cuts Forecast  NEW YORK (Reuters) - Diebold Inc. &lt;A HREF="http://www.investor.reuters.com/FullQuote.aspx?ticker=DBD.N target=/stocks/quickinfo/fullquote"&gt;DBD.N&lt;/A&gt;, the leading  maker of automated teller machines, on Monday reduced its  third-quarter and full-year earnings forecasts as it absorbs  costs for recertifying its electronic voting machines in  California and for expenses related to a pending civil action  in that state.
