Hicks Muse Pays for ConAgra's Swift Stake  NEW YORK (Reuters) - ConAgra Foods Inc. &lt;A HREF="http://www.investor.reuters.com/FullQuote.aspx?ticker=CAG.N target=/stocks/quickinfo/fullquote"&gt;CAG.N&lt;/A&gt; on Tuesday  said private equity firm Hicks, Muse, Tate   Furst Inc.  exercised its option to buy the company's minority stake in  Swift Foods, and that ConAgra received \$194 million in the  transaction.
