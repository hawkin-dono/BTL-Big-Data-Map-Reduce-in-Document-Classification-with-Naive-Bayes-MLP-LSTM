Geiberger Avoids Bogeys, Leads Greensboro (AP) AP - Brent Geiberger stayed out of trouble for a change. "That was probably one of the things holding me back," he said. "I might have three or four birdies and then three or four bogeys. Instead of having a nice round, you have a little sub-par round when everybody else is shooting 3 or 4 under."
